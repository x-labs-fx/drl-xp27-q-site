Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is DrL-Xp27-Q and how does it work?

DrL-Xp27-Q is an Expert Advisor (EA) for MT4 that uses deep‐reinforcement learning to trade EURUSD & AUDUSD. It continuously analyzes price movements and volatility in real time to decide entry, exit, and position size—aiming to maximize risk‐adjusted returns with low drawdown.

2. Do I need any coding or trading experience?

No coding is required. Simply download and install the EA, attach it to an EURUSD or AUDUSD chart, and enable “AutoTrading.” Basic familiarity with MT4 (opening charts, enabling automated trading) is all you need.

3. How do I start my free 7-day trial?

Click the Download Free 7-Day Trial button on the home page. No payment or license key is required. Install the DrL-Xp27-Q.ex4 file in MT4’s Experts folder, attach to a chart, and begin trading immediately.

4. What happens after my 7-day trial ends?

After 7 days, if you wish to continue using DrL-Xp27-Q (on demo or live), you pay a one-time license fee. This unlocks full access—no subscription or recurring charges under $10 000 live equity.

5. How does the “Pay Only If You Profit” model work?

Once your live account equity goes above $10 000, we keep 10 % of any new profits generated by DrL-Xp27-Q. If your equity remains under $10 000, there are no additional fees beyond the one-time $500.

6. Can I run this EA on a live account straight away?

Yes, but we strongly recommend testing on a demo account for a few weeks to confirm you’re comfortable with its behavior. After trial, you can run the same .ex4 on a live EURUSD or AUDUSD account.

7. What should I set “Equity Risk %” to?

The default is 2 %—meaning the EA will risk up to 2 % of your account equity on each new position. Lower numbers (e.g., 1 %) are more conservative; higher (e.g., 10 %) are more aggressive. Choose based on your risk tolerance.

8. What kind of drawdown should I expect?

On our tests, the max drawdown has hovered around 10-15 %. The EA actively reduces position size when volatility spikes to limit account drops. Keep in mind all trading carries risk, and live results can differ from demo.

9. Is technical/support help included?

Yes. During your free trial and afterwards, you have email support for setup or troubleshooting questions.

10. Does it require a VPS or can I run on my desktop?

You can run DrL-Xp27-Q on any machine with MT4 installed, though using a low-latency VPS can reduce slippage and ensure continuous 24/5 operation.
