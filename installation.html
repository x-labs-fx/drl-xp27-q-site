Installation & Setup Guide

Follow these steps to download, install, and configure the DrL-Xp27-Q EA in your MetaTrader 4 platform. By the end, you’ll be ready to run a 7-day free trial on demo or live.

1. Download the EA

  1. Click the Download Free 7-Day Trial button on the main page. This automatically fetches the latest .ex4 file from our GitHub releases.
  2. Save DrL-Xp27-Q.ex4 to a folder you can easily find (e.g., your Desktop or Downloads folder).

2. Copy to MT4 Experts Folder

  1. Open your MetaTrader 4 installation directory. If you’re on Windows, the default path is typically:
    C:\Program Files (x86)\MetaTrader 4\MQL4\Experts\
  2. Copy the downloaded DrL-Xp27-Q.ex4 file into …\MQL4\Experts\. You may need administrator privileges to write here.
  3. Restart MetaTrader 4 (if it was already open), or launch it if it’s closed. This ensures MT4 sees the new EA file.

3. Attach the EA to a Chart

  1. In MT4’s “Navigator” window (press Ctrl + N if hidden), expand Expert Advisors ➞ and find DrL-Xp27-Q.
  2. Open a new chart for either EURUSD or AUDUSD (preferably 1-hour or 4-hour timeframe). Right-click that symbol in “Market Watch” and choose Chart Window.
  3. Drag DrL-Xp27-Q from the “Navigator” onto the chart, or double-click it. A configuration dialog appears.

4. Configure Inputs

  1. In the Inputs tab, you will see only one variable: Equity Risk % (default 2.0). This setting controls how much of your account equity is risked per trade.
  2. Adjust “Equity Risk %” to match your personal risk tolerance. For example:
    • 5.0 → more conservative
    • 10.0 → default, balanced
    • 15.0 → more aggressive (larger positions)
  3. Click OK to save inputs.

5. Enable Automated Trading

  1. In the top toolbar of MT4, click the AutoTrading button so it turns green.
  2. Ensure the smiley face appears at the top‐right of your chart, indicating the EA is allowed to trade.

6. Monitor & Trade

  1. Once enabled, DrL-Xp27-Q will start analyzing market data (price, ATR, RSI, volatility) and placing trades automatically.
  2. You can view all open positions in the Trade tab of the Terminal window.
  3. If you wish to pause the EA at any time, simply click the AutoTrading button again to turn it red.

7. Switching to Live after Trial

  1. Your first 7 days are entirely free on demo—no payment needed. After that, to continue using on demo or live, click “Pay $500 & Download EA.”
  2. Enter your US bank/crypto payment according to the Payment Instructions page.
  3. The same DrL-Xp27-Q.ex4 file works for both demo and live accounts.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • If MT4 cannot find DrL-Xp27-Q.ex4, confirm the file is in MQL4\Experts\, then restart MT4.
  • If “AutoTrading” is off (red), the EA will not place trades—even if configured correctly.
  • Make sure your demo account has at least $1 000 balance for the EA to open initial positions.
  • If you see error messages in the “Experts” or “Journal” tabs of the Terminal window, take a screenshot and contact support@xlabsfx.com.
